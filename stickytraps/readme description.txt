This dataset contains yellow sticky trap with annotated insects.
MR	1312	NC	510	WF	5591
In total 1312 Macrolophus MR have been annotated
In total 510 Nesidiocoris NC have been annotated
In total 5591 Whiteflies WF have been annotated

Grand total of 7413 insects are annotated

The labels are in corresponding xml files

Labels have been generated with LabelImg
It is available at https://github.com/tzutalin/labelImg
